About Our Community

Building bridges between cultures, celebrating our heritage, and supporting each other.

Who We Are

The Friends of India Association Turkey is a thriving, diverse community of professionals, students, and families. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of belonging and keeping our incredible Indian traditions alive in the beautiful country of Turkey.

Our Mission

To create a robust support network for all Indians arriving and living in Turkey. We aim to ease the transition into Turkish life while providing a platform to celebrate major Indian festivals and cultural events together.

What We Do

From organizing Diwali galas and Holi celebrations to providing legal and social guidance through our Help Desk, we offer various avenues to connect, network, and grow.
